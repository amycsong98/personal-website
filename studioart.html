RULERS?
2018
Materials: wooden rulers, black paint, hot glue, tape, crayons, paper
Tools: illustrator, laser cutter
Description:
Using illustrator, I created templates and designs for the rulers. Then, I used the laser cutter to cut the rulers into different shapes and designs. I assembled them using hot glue and glue. Lastly, I had my colleages draw pictures using these rulers.
